Born and raised in Miami Beach, Elisa is a student at the University of Miami. Before attending UM, she spent time in Boulder and Denver, Colorado. She worked at the Denver Art Museum in the education department. Elisa helped establish "Arte Comunidad", an educational program targeted toward Denver's Latin community. On her free time Elisa enjoys going to concerts, visiting art museums, and adventuring outdoors.
